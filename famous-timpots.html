Famous Timpots

Timpots who have left their mark — in uniform, in laboratories and operating theatres, in boardrooms, on stages, on the field. A growing list, drawn from what alumni themselves have shared in the Timpots Forever group. Missing someone? Send their story to admin@timpotsforever.org.

Military & Uniformed Services

Surg. Vice Admiral Arti Sarin
ISC 1981 · Pluto Captain
Pluto
First woman officer to become Director General of Armed Forces Medical Services (DGAFMS). Earlier Director General Medical Services (Navy). Her service spans all three branches of the Indian Armed Forces — Army, Navy and Air Force.
Air Vice Marshal NM Samuel (Retd.)
ISC c. 1980
Retired Air Vice Marshal, Indian Air Force. Batchmate of Dr Meenakshi Anantram.
Surg. Rear Admiral Manish Honwad
Indian Navy
Recently appointed Additional Director General Medical Services (Navy) — under VAdm Arti Sarin.
Gp Capt Prabhakar Allam (Veteran)
Old School · 1962
Group Captain, Indian Air Force. Credits his school years for the discipline that shaped his journey into a military school and beyond.
Commodore J Sridhar
1987
Commodore, Indian Navy.
Commodore S Raghav
1988
Commodore, Indian Navy.
Commodore Saurabh Thakur
1993
Commodore, Indian Navy.
Capt. A. Nagaraj Subbarao, PhD
ICSE 1980 · Timpany 1969–1980
Master Mariner with over two decades commanding vessels across global waters. Now Dean, School of Commerce & Management Studies and Professor of Leadership & Strategy at Dayananda Sagar University. Holds two PhDs (Organisational Behaviour; Higher Education). Recipient of the CII Indian Change Maker Award, 2024.

Medicine & Sciences

Dr Priya Abraham
ISC 1981
Jupiter
One of India's leading virologists. Director of the National Institute of Virology, Pune. Known for her contributions to COVID-19 testing techniques and SARS-CoV-2 genome studies; a key figure at the PMO during the pandemic. A biopic on her life has been made.
Dr Vijay S. Gorantla, MD PhD FRCS
1987
Medical academic and reconstructive surgeon affiliated with the McGowan Institute for Regenerative Medicine, University of Pittsburgh.
Dr Smriti Chitra
ICSE c. 2010/11
Recipient of the DY Patil gold medal for MD.
Prof. Madhu
Class of 1974
Professor of Psychology, Andhra University. Pioneering work on the psychology of diabetes, beginning in 1990 with collaborators in the field. Passed away in the first wave of COVID-19, 2020.

Corporate & Finance Leaders

Purnachandra Rao
ISC 1974 · Mercury Captain
Mercury
Vice Chairman, Bank of America and Chairman of its Investment Banking Division. Based in New York. Began his Wall Street career at Merrill Lynch (which was acquired by Bank of America).
Keshav R Murugesh
ISC 1981

Harmit Singh

Chief Financial Officer, Levi Strauss & Co.
A. Rama Krishna
New York

Entrepreneurs & Business Owners

Kamal Sagar
Bangalore
Indian architect (IIT Kharagpur). Founder & Chairman, Total Environment Building Systems and Total Environment Hospitality. Designer, real-estate developer, restaurateur and music enthusiast.
Neelam Chhiber
NID graduate
Co-founder of Industree and Mother Earth; pioneer in building inclusive, regenerative livelihoods. Over 30 years empowering women from vulnerable communities through enterprise-led models. Recognised by the World Economic Forum and The Economic Times; independent director at CaratLane and Prestige Group. Through Mission Creative Million, she aims to impact three million producers by 2030.
Dr Meenakshi Anantram
ISC 1980 · Jupiter Captain
Jupiter
Founder & CEO of Razzmatazz, one of India's pioneering event-management companies — having hosted events for Presidents and global icons. Young Scientist Award recipient and the first Indian woman scientist nominated on the Antarctic Mission. National-level athlete (Indian handball as a Timpany student) and a Limca Book of Records awardee. Amazon #1 Best-Selling Author. Together with her husband, she is part of India's first palliative medical-clown couple, recognised with the Mahatma Gandhi Desh Sewa Puraskar on Republic Day 2025. TEDx speaker.
V J Gupta
Std 11–12 · 1978–80
Third-generation proprietor of Gupta Brothers — the Vizag bookshop and stationery institution that opened in 1916 and is still trading, having long since crossed its centenary. Generations of Timpany students bought their textbooks here every June.

Arts & Entertainment

Ramana Gogula
ISC 1976 · Pluto Captain
Pluto
Indo-American entrepreneur, venture capitalist, music composer and singer of Telugu film music and Indian pop. Notable film compositions include Premante Idera (1998), Thammudu (1999), Badri (2000), Johnny (2003), Lakshmi (2006), Annavaram (2006) and Yogi (2007), with four collaborations alongside Pawan Kalyan. Currently Vice President, Clean Technology Innovation at Stanley Black & Decker, Inc. and venture partner at Anthill Ventures. Sang Timpany's 70th-anniversary anthem.
Sonika Mahajan (Chopra)
Mumbai
Jupiter
Bollywood actress and model.
Badri Chavan
Mumbai
Actor and public figure (Hindi film and television).
Keya Hatkar
Recent Timpot
A recent young Timpot already making her own mark — among other things, she hand-crafted the memorial video for Jazz that opened the April 2026 remembrance service.

Sport

Damu (Damodar)
Class of 1970 · Neptune Captain
Neptune
Andhra State Schools Cricket Captain in the Gulam Ahmad Trophy, 1969. School champion four years running (1967–70); Neptune House Captain 1969 and 1970, leading Neptune to its first championship in 1969 — a streak that continued through 1972.
