What this is, and isn’t

Timpots @ Work is a community service — not a paid directory, not a referral business, not affiliated with any commercial platform. We do not collect fees, take commissions, or rank listings by sponsorship. Listings are vetted only for alumni status and basic accuracy. Quality of work, prices, and any business dealings are entirely between you and the listed alumnus.

Removal. Email admin@timpotsforever.org to update or remove your listing at any time.

See also our Non-Commercial Purpose in the Terms & Conditions.

Ground rules

  • Timpots only. One listing per business. Real businesses, honest claims.
  • Nothing illegal, predatory, or aimed at minors — no MLMs, gambling, adult content, or unlicensed regulated services.
  • We review every listing. We can decline, edit, or remove any listing at any time.
  • Inclusion isn’t endorsement. We don’t vouch for quality — deal directly with the listed Timpot.
  • No spamming. Don’t use the directory to harvest, pitch, or market to fellow alumni.
  • Easy to leave. Update, remove, or report a concern by emailing admin@timpotsforever.org.
