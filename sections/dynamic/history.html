Our Story · Since 1931

A history worth carrying forward.

Timpany School was founded in 1931 by Reverend A.E. Timpany of the Canadian Baptist Mission, first in Kakinada and later in Vizag. From a single small room with ten students to the landmark institution in Asilmetta we know today, the school has shaped generations of Vizagites. Explore the archive — founding documents, photographs of the old campus, the 1964 move to Waltair, class photos across the decades, and the voices of alumni who carry Timpany with them across the world.
