Where we’re headed

And we’re just getting started.

What you see today is the first chapter. The working groups are quietly building toward a much richer home for Timpots — a place where you can find each other, learn from each other, and help each other along.

  • The Jeyaraja Rao Memorial Debate Competition — inaugural year July 4, 2026, with travel, coaching, and accommodation provided to participating schools across Andhra Pradesh.
  • Spotlights on prominent alumni and the teachers who shaped us.
  • A Career Corner — for established Timpots and the younger ones just starting out.
  • A searchable Alumni Directory — already live for signed-in members; the global Timpots Worldwide map is open to all.
  • A Professional Services Corner — Timpots offering services to other Timpots, around the world.
  • Social channels — Facebook, YouTube, WhatsApp — coming soon.
  • Vlogs & podcasts with prominent alumni and community leaders.
  • And much more — as Timpots tell us what they want to see next.

None of this happens without you.
