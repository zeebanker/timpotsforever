Dedication

Honouring Jazz.

This website for thousands of Timpots — students, teachers and staff — honours the legacy of Mercy Jeya Raja Rao, or Jazz as we called her. This is where we gather her stories, our stories, letters, photographs, and the lessons she left behind. It's also where Timpots share memories, photos and videos of our time at Timpany, and where we plan reunions and Timpot events. And in the spirit of service Jazz instilled in us, it's where we come together to give back to the communities where we live and work.

This is your website — explore, contribute, add to it, and connect with your friends and Timpots worldwide. That's what Jazz would have wanted us to do.
