Dedication

Honouring Jazz.

This website is for thousands of Timpots — students, teachers, and staff — and honours the legacy of Mercy Jeya Raja Rao, our Jazz. We dedicate this to her with respect and everlasting gratitude.

This is a place to gather her stories and ours: letters, photographs, memories, and the lessons she left behind. It is also where we Timpots can share photos and videos from our time at Timpany, reconnect with old friends, and plan reunions.

And in the spirit of service that Jazz instilled in us, it is where we come together to give back to the communities where we work and live.

This is your website. Explore it, contribute to it, improve it, add your memories, and connect with Timpots around the world.

That is what Jazz would have wanted us to do. That's how we can keep her legacy alive.
