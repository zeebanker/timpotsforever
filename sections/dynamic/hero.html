How It Began

Out of a memorial, a movement.

When Jazz passed on March 22, 2026, Timpots came home — to the memorial in Vizag, to gatherings across the world, to a livestream from wherever they were. Out of those weeks of mourning, three alumni groups quietly formed: one builds this website, one is launching The Timpany Times — our bi-monthly newsletter — and one organizes events like the Jeyaraja Rao Memorial Debate Competition. Jazz's work, continued.
