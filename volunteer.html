It takes a village.

Timpots Forever is built by Timpots, for Timpots. Every story, every photo, every reunion happens because someone said yes. Here’s how you can lend a hand.

Out of Jazz’s memorial came a movement — alumni quietly forming three working groups to keep her legacy alive: one builds and maintains this website, one is launching The Timpany Times, our bi-monthly newsletter, and one organises events like the Jeyaraja Rao Memorial Debate Competition. The work is small, ongoing, and collective.

There’s no minimum commitment — some Timpots help with a single photo or memory; others stay on for years. Tick whichever roles interest you below and we’ll be in touch about what fits your time and skills.

Ways to help

Fact-check history & stories

Read what’s on the site, spot what we got wrong, send us what you remember. Especially helpful if you were there.

Photo archive & identification

Share old photographs from your years at Timpany — class pictures, plays, sports days. Help us name the faces and dates we don’t recognise.

Web team

Help update and maintain this website — content edits, new pages, design tweaks. Coding skill welcome, not required.

Newsletter contributor

Write or pitch pieces for The Timpany Times. Memoirs, profiles, photo essays, opinion pieces — anything a Timpot would want to read.

Ambassador & outreach

Bring more Timpots into the fold. Reconnect old batches, post on social, organise informal gatherings wherever you are in the world.

Giving-back campaign lead

Spearhead a cause: a scholarship drive, an annual fundraiser, a community project. Bring the idea; we’ll bring the network.

Thank you

We’ve got your note. Someone from the working group that matches your interests will be in touch — usually within a week or two. In the meantime, you can email admin@timpotsforever.org any time.
